aromatherapy?

Clinical Aromatherapy is the use of essential oils to target a specific problem and have a measurable result. It is not the use of essential oils to make soaps, beauty products, or perfumes. When you use an essential oil blend to ease a strained muscle or to facilitate a good night's sleep, you are using Clinical Aromatherapy.
