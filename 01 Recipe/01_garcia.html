How To Make Baked Buffalo Wings

If there is one thing I know, It's that buffalo wings are delicious. But sadly like most good food, wings are awfully unhealthy. Most people deep fry their wings, though. By baking your wings instead, you get the same great taste, but a lot less guilt! Down below you will find one of my favorite recipes, which can be found on Allrecipes.com.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 20 chicken wings
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1/2 cup hot pepper sauce(your choice)

If you are vegan, Whole Foods has an amazing selection of vegan friendly ingredients!

Method

  1. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil, and lightly grease with cooking spray.
  2. Place the flour, cayenne pepper, garlic powder, and salt into a resealable plastic bag, and shake to mix.
  3. Add the chicken wings, seal, and toss until well coated with the flour mixture.
  4. Place the wings onto the prepared baking sheet, and place into the refrigerator. Refrigerate at least 1 hour.
  5. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  6. Whisk together the melted butter and hot sauce in a small bowl.
  7. Dip the wings into the butter mixture, and place back on the baking sheet.
  8. Bake in preheated oven until the chicken is no longer pink in the center, and crispy on the outside, about 45 minutes.
  9. Turn the wings over halfway during cooking so they cook evenly.
  10. Enjoy!
